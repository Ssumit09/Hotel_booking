HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

FAQ'S

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Our check-in time is 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM.

Do you provide airport transportation?

Yes, we offer airport transportation services for our guests. Please contact our front desk to arrange for pick-up.

Can I make a hotel booking over the phone?

Yes. Simply give us a call on 022 4164 2214 (Monday to Sunday from 09:00 am till 07:00 pm).

How do I know if my hotel booking was successful?

After the payment process is completed, you will receive a confirmation email.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, we offer complimentary breakfast for all our guests.

Can I request a late check-out?

Late check-out requests are subject to availability. Please check with the front desk on the day of your departure.
